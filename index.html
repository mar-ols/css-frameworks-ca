Who are we?

We at introverti wanted to make a calmer social media space for the introverts, neurodivergents and quiet thinkers of the world

On our site you can:

- Share images, videos or text posts

- Join rl-meet ups/activities with like-minded people, with no expectations of small-talk

- Host or join a Zoom call for body doubling

You'll find:

- No ads

- No annoying auto scroll

- No auto play on videos

- Social battery status on your profile with option to turn off messages/chat if you're running on low
